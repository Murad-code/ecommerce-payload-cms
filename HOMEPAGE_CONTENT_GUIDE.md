# Homepage Content Guide for Marquee Ecommerce Store

This guide explains how to set up your homepage content in the Payload CMS admin panel and what content to use for each section.

## Accessing the Homepage

1. Log into your Payload CMS admin panel (usually at `/admin`)
2. Navigate to **Content** â†’ **Pages**
3. Find or create a page with the slug `home` (this is your homepage)
4. The page structure has three tabs: **Hero**, **Content**, and **SEO**

---

## 1. HERO SECTION (First Tab)

The hero is the large banner at the top of your homepage. Choose **High Impact** for maximum visual appeal.

### Hero Type: **High Impact** (Recommended)

**Settings:**
- **Type**: Select "High Impact"
- **Rich Text**: This is your main headline and subheading
- **Links**: Add call-to-action buttons
- **Media**: Upload a stunning background image

### Content Suggestions:

**Rich Text (Main Headline):**
```
Premium Marquees for Every Occasion

Transform your events with our professional-grade marquees. From intimate garden parties to grand celebrations, we have the perfect marquee solution for you.
```

**Links (Call-to-Action Buttons):**
- Button 1: "Browse Marquees" â†’ Link to `/products` or `/shop`
- Button 2: "Get a Quote" â†’ Link to contact form or `/contact`

**Media (Background Image):**
- Upload a high-quality image of a beautiful marquee setup
- Suggested: Outdoor event with marquee, well-lit, showing happy guests
- Image should be at least 1920x1080px for best quality

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Images**: Use high-quality, professional photos of marquees in use
2. **Consistency**: Keep your tone professional but friendly
3. **CTAs**: Include clear call-to-action buttons throughout
4. **Mobile**: All content will be responsive, but preview on mobile devices
5. **Testing**: Use the "Preview" feature in Payload CMS before publishing
6. **Publishing**: Make sure to set status to "Published" when ready

---
